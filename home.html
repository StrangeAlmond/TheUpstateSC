Welcome to TheUpstateSC.com

Your collaborative local guide to life, history, and culture in Upstate South Carolina.

 

What is this?

TheUpstateSC.com is a community-powered wiki dedicated to documenting everything about Upstate South Carolina—from local history and neighborhoods to parks, events, hidden gems, and helpful resources for both locals and newcomers.

Whether you're exploring the Blue Ridge foothills, researching Greenville’s transformation, or looking for the best coffee in Spartanburg, this is your hub.

 

What can you find here?

Explore pages on:

Cities & Towns – From Greenville to Gaffney

Local History – Events, landmarks, and forgotten stories

Parks & Nature – Trails, rivers, and natural areas

Culture & Traditions – Local festivals, food, and southern quirks

Businesses & Institutions – Shops, schools, libraries, churches

How to Live Here – Moving guides, transportation, utilities, job help

Community Spotlights – Notable people, grassroots groups, and stories

 

Want to contribute?

This wiki grows through community knowledge. You can:

  • Create new pages on towns, places, or topics you care about
  • Update existing information with recent changes
  • Add photos, history, or personal insights
  • Help organize, fact-check, or clean up pages
