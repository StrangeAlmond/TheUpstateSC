Spartanburg

“Service is our business”

Spartanburg, SC is the 13th most populous city in the state of South Carolina. It was incorporated in 1831, but its history extends back to 1753 when a treaty with the Cherokee Indian Nation opened up the area for settlers. Spartanburg County was formed in 1785. Its name stems from the Spartan Regiment, a militia unit which participated in the Revolutionary War.
