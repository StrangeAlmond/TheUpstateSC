Cities & Towns of Upstate South Carolina

Explore the diverse cities, towns, and communities that make up the heart of the Upstate.

Upstate South Carolina is home to a wide range of communities—from bustling cities like Greenville and Spartanburg, to small towns rich with history, charm, and local character. This page serves as a hub for discovering them all.

Counties in the Upstate

Upstate South Carolina includes the following counties:

  • Greenville County
  • Spartanburg County
  • Anderson County
  • Pickens County
  • Oconee County
  • Laurens County
  • Cherokee County
  • Union County
  • Greenwood County

Each county page contains a breakdown of its cities, towns, and unincorporated communities.
